about us - retrobites burger
karterl unlimited

Welcome to Retrobites Burger Kartel Unlimited! Step into our
culinary kartel, where we have been serving customers delicious
and affordable food since 2022. Our menu doesn't just only offer
burgers, but also offer many of your favorite comfort foods
such as chicken wings, french fries, takoyaki, pizza and more!
All with many different flavors to choose from! Select from our
unlimited all you can eat packages when you dine in or order
ala carte when you take out or order for delivery. Come visit us here
at our main branch at Retrobites in Taguig City or at our newly
franchised location at Jazzy n' Jin Foobhub in Mabalacat City.
